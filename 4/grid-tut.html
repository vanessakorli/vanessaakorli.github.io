Lost in Amsterdam: Top 10 Places to Visit

After living in the Netherlands for a year, I am definitely not qualified to be recommeding anything to anyone, but this is a summary of my favourite places that I got to briefly explore during my time. Some of these are season dependent, but that's just a great excuse to visit multiple times!

10.Arnhem

Netherlands Open Air Museum, beautiful station and centre

9. Delft

Botanical Gardens, Prinsenhof, modern architecture

8. Maastricht

Maas river, Bonnefanten, Bureau gorgeous French inspired architecture

7. Zwolle

Melkmarkt, live music festivals, beautiful 18th century architecture, Hedon

6. Leiden

Museumplein, jazz festival (Jan)

5. Rotterdam

Cube houses, street art

4. Eindhoven

Light festival (Nov), incredible food, strijp-S, philips stadion, Evoluon-close to Den Bosch

3. Den Haag

Tweede Kamer, chinatown, flea markets, canal

2. Amsterdam

Jordaan, canals, food markets, flea markets (Ijhallen), A'dam lookout, everything you could want in a city-close to Haarlem, Zaandam

1. Utrecht

Gorgeous canals, square of cathedrals and beautiful buildings, park has events constantly, Hoog Catharijn, TivoliVredenburg, Bibliotheek Neude-art festival(Oct)
